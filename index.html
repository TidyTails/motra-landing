The Problem

Autonomous vehicles never stop. Neither should their care.

No Driver, No Eyes

With no human driver, there's no one to notice spills, trash, or wear between rides. Passengers expect a clean vehicle every time.

Scaling Crisis

Waymo alone does 400,000+ rides per week. Traditional depot-based cleaning can't keep up with distributed, 24/7 fleets.

Fixed Cost Trap

In-house operations mean expensive facilities, full-time staff, and capacity that doesn't flex with demand.

Critical Sensors

Dirty sensors = degraded driving. AV maintenance isn't just cosmetic—it's safety-critical.

The Solution

Mobile fleet care, on demand

MOTRA deploys a network of trained technicians directly to vehicles—wherever they are. No depots. No downtime. Just clean, maintained, ready-to-ride autonomy.

  • Between-ride cleaning in under 10 minutes
  • Deep cleaning and detailing
  • Sensor cleaning and calibration checks
  • Light maintenance and fluid top-offs
  • Emergency spill and biohazard response
