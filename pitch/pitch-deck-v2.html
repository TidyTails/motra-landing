What We Do

MOTRA is the infrastructure layer for autonomous vehicle fleet care.

07 / WHY NOT IN-HOUSE

The Objection

Why won't Waymo just do this themselves?

1. They Already Outsource

Waymo uses Transdev, Moove, Avis, TechForce, Amerit, and Terawatt for fleet services today.

Build vs. buy decision: they buy.

2. Not Core Competency

Waymo's edge is autonomous driving software. Every dollar on cleaning is a dollar not spent on AI.

They want a vendor, not a department.

3. Math Doesn't Work

20+ cities = thousands of employees, facilities, HR, training, benefits.

Gig model = variable cost. They pay per clean.

Uber just validated this. If the big players could do this better in-house, they wouldn't be building outsourced solutions.
