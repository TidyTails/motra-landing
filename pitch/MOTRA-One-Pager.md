## The Opportunity

Waymo just hit **400,000 rides/week** and is targeting **1 million/week by end of 2026**. They raised **$16 billion** at a **$126 billion valuation**. The robotaxi industry has reached escape velocity.

But here's the problem: **robotaxis run 15+ hours a day**, and someone has to clean them between rides. Right now, Waymo uses **1,000+ Transdev operators** at centralized depots. That doesn't scale to 20+ cities and millions of rides.

---

## The Solution

**MOTRA is the gig-powered infrastructure layer for autonomous vehicle fleet care.**

We deploy mobile technicians directly to vehicles — wherever they are — for cleaning, light maintenance, and emergency response. Think DoorDash for fleet maintenance.

| Service | Price | Time |
|---------|-------|------|
| Quick Clean | $12-18 | 5-10 min |
| Deep Clean | $45-75 | 30-60 min |
| Emergency | $75-150 | On-demand |
